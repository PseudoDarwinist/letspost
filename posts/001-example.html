AI Agent for Customer Support

Problem

Customer support teams are overwhelmed with repetitive questions that could be automated.

System Architecture

An AI agent that uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to answer customer questions based on documentation.

Solution

The AI agent processes customer queries, searches the knowledge base, and provides accurate responses in real-time.

Examples

  • Answering product documentation questions
  • Troubleshooting common technical issues
  • Routing complex issues to human agents
